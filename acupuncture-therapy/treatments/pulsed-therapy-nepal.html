What exactly is Pulsed Therapy?

In simple terms, Pulsed Therapy is an innovative treatment that uses gentle, rhythmic electrical or magnetic pulses to stimulate your muscles and nerves. It has proven to be highly effective not just for relieving pain, but also for managing various mental health conditions.

Here at Maria Hospital's Acupuncture & Therapy department, we focus on two main types: Electroacupuncture and Repetitive Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (often called rTMS).

The Types of Pulsed Therapy We Offer

1. Electroacupuncture

Think of this as a modern upgrade to traditional acupuncture. Here is how the process works:

  • A trained specialist carefully inserts very fine, sterile needles into specific points on your body.
  • We then apply a very mild, completely safe electrical current through those needles to give the healing effect a natural boost.
  • The Benefits: It is great for relieving pain, regulating your appetite, curbing cravings, and even improving how your body processes fat.
  • The Experience: You simply get to rest comfortably for 20 to 30 minutes while the gentle pulses do the work.

2. Repetitive Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (rTMS)

If you prefer a treatment without needles, rTMS is a fantastic non-invasive alternative.

  • We gently place an electromagnetic coil against your scalp, which sends small magnetic pulses to specific cells in your brain.
  • The Benefits: This is an officially approved treatment for major depression, and it is especially helpful if standard medications or talk therapy haven't quite worked out. It is also used to treat OCD, migraines, and insomnia, and it can even help you quit smoking.
  • The Experience: The process is painless and focuses entirely on stimulating the right nerves to help improve your memory, focus, and overall emotional health.

What to Expect During Your Visit

Your comfort and safety are always our top priorities at Maria Hospital. Both of these therapies are simple outpatient procedures, meaning there is no surgery and absolutely no anesthesia required.

  • For Electroacupuncture: You will lie down and get comfortable. You might feel a gentle, rhythmic tingling or a mild ache, but this is completely normal.
  • For rTMS: You will relax in a comfortable chair and wear earplugs. You will hear a clicking sound and feel a rapid tapping on your scalp. The sessions are quick, lasting anywhere from 3.5 to 20 minutes, and you stay fully awake the whole time.
  • After your session: You can get right back to your normal daily routine in Nepal, and you can even drive yourself home immediately after the appointment.

Is Pulsed Therapy Safe?

Absolutely. These treatments are incredibly safe and well-tolerated by our patients.

  • Electroacupuncture relies on safe, certified sterile needles. While rare, minor side effects like temporary pain or light-headedness can occasionally happen.
  • rTMS completely avoids surgical risks and does not cause memory loss. You might experience mild side effects like a temporary headache or a tingling feeling on your scalp, but these usually fade very quickly as you continue your sessions.

Important Note: Because rTMS relies on strong magnetic fields, it isn't the right fit for everyone. If you have any metal medical devices implanted in your body, like a pacemaker or a cochlear implant, please let our doctors know right away during your consultation.
