What is Acupoint Injection Therapy?

Think of it as the perfect blend of traditional acupuncture and modern medicine. Instead of the usual dry needles, our trained professionals use a very fine needle to inject a tiny amount of sterile, healing liquid straight into specific acupuncture points or tight muscle knots.

This gives you a powerful, two-in-one healing effect:

  • Immediate Pain Relief: The gentle needle placement triggers your nervous system to naturally block pain signals right away.
  • Long-Lasting Healing: The injected fluid acts like a "liquid needle." It stays in your tissue to keep stimulating the healing point, delivering concentrated medicine exactly where your body needs it the most.

What exactly is injected?

During your doctor consultation, we will figure out the best solution for your specific needs. Your safety is our priority, so we only use natural, sterile, and highly beneficial fluids. Depending on what your body needs, this might include:

  • Vitamins and Minerals: Solutions like Vitamin B12 or Vitamin D to boost your immune system, support healthy nerves, and fight fatigue.
  • Natural and Herbal Remedies: Safe, natural formulas that are great for bringing down inflammation, healing joints, and speeding up injury recovery.
  • Hydrating Fluids: Simple saline (saltwater) or sugar water to deeply hydrate and relax chronically cramped muscles.
  • Mild Anesthetics: A tiny bit of a mild numbing medicine (like lidocaine) to ensure the process is completely comfortable and to give you instant relief from severe muscle knots.

Just a quick note: this is a very gentle therapy. We do not use heavy IV drips, harsh steroids, or addictive medications.

What conditions can this treatment help?

AIT is surprisingly versatile. We commonly use it to treat a wide range of issues, including:

  • Chronic and Acute Pain: It is highly effective at tackling stubborn back aches, stiff necks, knee osteoarthritis, and frozen shoulders.
  • Post-Surgery Discomfort: It helps patients recover more comfortably by offering fast relief from pain and nausea, completely avoiding the dizziness that often comes with standard painkillers.
  • Cancer Treatment Support: It works wonderfully to ease the harsh side effects of chemotherapy by reducing nerve pain, nausea, and severe fatigue.
  • Sleep Issues: It can calm your nervous system to naturally improve your sleep quality and help with insomnia, all without the daytime grogginess caused by sleeping pills.

Is Acupoint Injection safe?

Absolutely. When done by our certified experts, Acupoint Injection Therapy is very safe. We use ultra-fine needles, so any discomfort is minimal.

Side effects are extremely rare and always mild. You might notice a little temporary redness or minor swelling at the injection site, but this usually clears up quickly on its own without needing any extra medical attention.
