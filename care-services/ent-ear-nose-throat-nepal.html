ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat) Care

Maria Hospital’s ENT department is here to help you with ear, nose, and throat problems. We use modern equipment and safe treatments to help you breathe, hear, and speak clearly again.

We treat everyone—from young children to older adults—in a friendly and comfortable setting.

Ear Care

  • Ear Infections (Acute and Chronic)
  • Ear Pain
  • Hearing Loss and Age-Related Hearing Problems
  • Ear Discharge
  • Ear Wax (Cerumen) Blockage
  • Tinnitus (Buzzing Sounds in the Ear)
  • Vertigo and Balance Disorders
  • Tympanic Membrane Perforation (Perforated Eardrum)
  • Otitis Media and Otitis Externa
  • Middle Ear Effusion (Secretory Otitis Media), Especially Common in Children

Nose and Sinus Care

  • Sinusitis (Acute and Chronic)
  • Nasal Allergies and Allergic Rhinitis
  • Frequent Sneezing, Runny Nose, and Nasal Congestion
  • Deviated Nasal Septum (DNS)
  • Nasal Polyps
  • Recurrent Nosebleeds (Epistaxis)
  • Loss or Decreased Sense of Smell

Throat Care

  • Tonsillitis and Recurrent Sore Throat
  • Enlarged Adenoids in Children
  • Throat Infections and Difficulty Swallowing
  • Hoarseness
  • Vocal Cord Nodules and Polyps
  • Oral Ulcers and Oral Lesions
  • Enlarged or Lumpy Lymph Nodes in the Neck (Initial ENT Assessment)

For persistent colds, nasal congestion, earaches, or throat problems, the ENT specialists at Maria Hospital in Nepal are ready to help.
