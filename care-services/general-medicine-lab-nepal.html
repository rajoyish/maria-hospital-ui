Laboratory Services

We provide a wide range of diagnostic tests to help doctors understand your health. We work closely with other departments to ensure your reports are accurate and ready when needed.

Our tests include:

  • Diabetes & Sugar Checks: Blood Glucose (RBS, Fasting, PP), and HbA1c.
  • Liver & Kidney Health:
    • LFT (Liver Function Test): Bilirubin, SGPT/ALT, SGOT/AST, Protein, Albumin, and AIP.
    • RFT (Renal Function Test): Urea, Creatinine, Sodium, Potassium, Uric Acid, and Calcium.
  • Heart & Cholesterol (Lipid Profile): Total Cholesterol, Triglycerides (TG), HDL, and LDL.
  • Infection & STD Screening: HIV 1/2 Rapid, HCV Rapid, HBsAg (Infection & Carrier/Immunity), VDRL RPR, and TPHA.
  • Hormones & Thyroid: Testosterone, T3, T4, and TSH.
  • General Health Tests:
    • Blood Grouping
    • Urine Tests (RE/ME and Pregnancy Test)
    • Semen Analysis
    • Absolute Eosinophil Count
    • Coagulation Tests
  • Other Procedures: Small Biopsy services.
