Anorectal/Piles at Maria Hospital, Nepal

The Anorectal/Piles department at Maria Hospital specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases affecting the anus, rectum, and lower colon. While these conditions can be painful or embarrassing, they are very common and can usually be managed effectively with proper medical intervention.

Our specialists provide gentle, effective care with a dedicated focus on pain management, rapid recovery, and patient privacy.

Conditions We Treat

We offer comprehensive care for a wide range of anorectal and hemorrhoidal conditions, including:

  • Hemorrhoids: Internal and external.
  • Anal Fissures: Painful tears or lacerations at the anal opening.
  • Anal Fistulas: Abnormal passages connecting the anal canal to the skin.
  • Perianal Abscesses: An accumulation of pus near the anus.
  • Rectal Prolapse: A condition where the rectum protrudes from the anus.
  • Pilonidal Sinus: A small tract or cyst near the coccyx (tailbone).
  • Chronic Constipation: Specifically cases involving anorectal complications.
  • Pruritus Ani: Persistent anal itching and irritation.
  • Painful Defecation: Difficulty or pain during bowel movements.
  • Rectal Bleeding: Specifically associated with anorectal diseases.
  • Benign Tumors & Polyps: Initial examination and specialized treatment.

Treatment Options

Based on your diagnosis, we provide tailored treatment plans including:

Conservative Management
Lifestyle and dietary recommendations to manage and prevent constipation.
Medical Therapy
Specialized medications and ointments for early-stage hemorrhoids, fissures, or infections.
Surgical Intervention
Minimally invasive procedures and traditional surgery, supplemented by advanced pain control and comprehensive follow-up care.

If you experience bleeding during bowel movements, localized pain, or chronic constipation, please do not ignore these symptoms. Visit the Anorectal/Piles department at Maria Hospital for a timely diagnosis and relief.
