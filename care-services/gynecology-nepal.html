Family Planning & Contraception

We offer comprehensive family planning services to help you make informed decisions about your reproductive health. Our specialists provide safe, confidential consultations to find the best contraceptive method for your body and lifestyle.

Copper T (Non-Hormonal IUD)

The Copper T is a highly effective, 100% hormone-free intrauterine device (IUD) that provides long-lasting pregnancy prevention (up to 10 years). Inserted as a quick outpatient procedure by our expert gynecologists, it is an excellent "fit-and-forget" solution.

  • 100% Hormone-Free: No synthetic hormones, meaning zero risk of hormone-related weight gain or mood changes.
  • Highly Effective & Long-Lasting: Provides continuous protection for 5 to 10 years.
  • Immediately Reversible: If you decide to plan for a pregnancy, your fertility returns to normal immediately after the device is removed by a doctor.
  • Low Maintenance: No daily pills to remember or monthly pharmacy visits.

Gynecological Cosmetic Care

We offer specialized cosmetic and reconstructive gynecological procedures designed to improve both function and aesthetics, enhancing your comfort and confidence.

  • Vaginal Tightening: Generally refers to non-surgical, energy-based treatments, such as localized CO2 laser or radiofrequency therapies, targeting the mucosal tissue lining the internal vaginal canal. The primary purpose is to stimulate natural collagen and elastin production to address mild tissue laxity, improve lubrication, and enhance overall vaginal tissue health without the risks or downtime associated with invasive surgery.
  • Labiaplasty: A surgical procedure targeting the external genitalia, specifically the labia minora (inner lips) or labia majora (outer lips). Its primary purpose is both aesthetic and functional, aiming to reduce the size or alter the shape of the labia to alleviate physical discomfort, such as chafing or pulling during physical activities, and to improve the overall cosmetic appearance of the vulva.
  • Clitoral Hood Reduction: A surgical intervention focused on the external fold of skin surrounding and protecting the clitoris, known as the prepuce. The primary purpose of this procedure is aesthetic refinement, often performed in conjunction with a labiaplasty to maintain visual proportion and prevent the clitoral hood from appearing overly prominent once the surrounding labial tissue has been reduced.
  • Vaginoplasty: A reconstructive surgical procedure targeting the internal vaginal canal and underlying pelvic floor muscles. The primary purpose is functional restoration, specifically repairing the muscle laxity and separation that often result from vaginal childbirth or the natural aging process, thereby enhancing muscle tone, structural support, and friction during intercourse.
  • Perineoplasty: A surgical repair targeting the perineum, which is the specific area of skin and muscle located between the vaginal opening and the anus. Its primary purpose is structural and functional reconstruction, designed to correct scarring, damage, or looseness typically caused by perineal tearing or episiotomies during childbirth, ultimately rebuilding the supportive foundation of the lower vaginal opening.
  • Hymenoplasty: A reconstructive surgical procedure targeting the hymen, the thin, delicate membrane situated at the opening of the vagina. The primary purpose of this surgery is the physical restoration of the hymen to simulate its intact, pre-ruptured state, a procedure most frequently sought by patients for profound personal, cultural, or religious reasons.
