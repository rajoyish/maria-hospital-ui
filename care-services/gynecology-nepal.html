Gynecology Care

Our goal is to help women in Nepal access reliable gynecological services, timely diagnosis, modern treatment options, and proactive family planning in a safe hospital environment.

Family Planning & Contraception

We offer comprehensive family planning services to help you make informed decisions about your reproductive health. Our specialists provide safe, confidential consultations to find the best contraceptive method for your body and lifestyle.

Copper T (Non-Hormonal IUD)

The Copper T is a highly effective, 100% hormone-free intrauterine device (IUD) that provides long-lasting pregnancy prevention (up to 10 years). Inserted as a quick outpatient procedure by our expert gynecologists, it is an excellent "fit-and-forget" solution.

  • 100% Hormone-Free: No synthetic hormones, meaning zero risk of hormone-related weight gain or mood changes.
  • Highly Effective & Long-Lasting: Provides continuous protection for 5 to 10 years.
  • Immediately Reversible: If you decide to plan for a pregnancy, your fertility returns to normal immediately after the device is removed by a doctor.
  • Low Maintenance: No daily pills to remember or monthly pharmacy visits.
