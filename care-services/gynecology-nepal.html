Gynecology Care

Maria Hospital's Gynecology Department provides comprehensive healthcare for women from puberty to menopause and beyond. Our male and female gynecologists are committed to providing every woman with respectful, confidential, and caring medical services.

Our goal is to help women in Nepal access reliable gynecological services, timely diagnosis, and modern treatment options in a safe hospital environment.

Gynecological Treatments and Conditions

  • Menstrual Irregularities: Menstrual Disorders
  • Menorrhagia: Excessive Menstrual Bleeding or Prolonged Periods
  • Dysmenorrhea: Painful Menstruation
  • Delayed Menstruation or Amenorrhea
  • Ovarian Cysts
  • Pelvic Pain
  • Vaginal Infections:
    • Yeast Infections
    • Bacterial Vaginosis
    • Trichomoniasis
  • Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs): In collaboration with the Urology/STD department
  • Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID)
  • Cervical Erosion and Cervicitis
  • Abnormal Vaginal Discharge
  • Dyspareunia: Painful Intercourse
  • Infertility: Assessment and Referral to Advanced Fertility Treatment
  • Premenstrual Syndrome (PMS) and Hormone-Related Mood Changes
  • Menopausal Issues: Hot Flashes, Menstrual Irregularities
  • Female Urinary Incontinence: In collaboration with Urology department

Other Women's Health Services

  • Family Planning Counseling and Contraception Advice

If you are experiencing any issues related to your menstrual cycle, vaginal health, pelvic pain, or fertility, the gynecology department at Maria Hospital in Nepal is dedicated to providing you with safe and respectful medical care.
